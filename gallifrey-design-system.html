Tat Svam Asi (तत्त्वमसि)

The ultimate Vedantic truth - "Thou art That." In our design system, this means the dissolution of boundaries between user and interface. When design achieves perfect transparency, the user becomes one with the system.

Moksha (मोक्ष)

Liberation from operational burden through seamless design that dissolves the illusion of complexity. Every element serves to free users from friction, achieving digital enlightenment.

Flow State

Frictionless user experiences that achieve perfect balance. Where technology becomes transparent and users enter states of effortless interaction with infinite possibility.

Ubuntu

Collective harmony in design - "I am because we are." User and system as one consciousness, where individual success creates collective prosperity through shared understanding.

Nirvana

Perfect visual balance between spiritual depth and technical excellence. The cessation of design friction, where form and function unite in ultimate harmony.

Gallifrey Wisdom

Time Lord consciousness applied to digital realms. Regenerative systems that evolve and transcend, creating infrastructure that manages itself across all dimensions of time and space.
